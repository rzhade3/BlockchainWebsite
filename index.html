Applications

Jericho is most useful for large scale internetworks
with fast moving data. Use cases include:

  • Industrial IoT Devices

    Enable autonomy among your sensors utilizing a P2P network of devices rather than a massive server farm. No more single points of failure.

  • Home Automation

    Need redundancy in your home network? Worried about data integrity or network intrusion? Jericho has built in data integrity and redundancy measures

  • Forward Facing Databases

    Jericho can secure your database from unauthorized data manipulation, protecting your data and your clients

  • Version Control

    Jericho keeps track of every change made to the network, making it trivial to restore any changes or detect unauthorized changes
