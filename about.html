About Us

Our Mission

All too often we hear tales of data loss or data manipulation, and in many cases there is no recourse. In some cases, it might be due to developer oversight, but in others it could be far more insidous. Using offline backups might be a good temporary solution, however these backups are not 100% current, and might lead to server downtime while replacing your database.

Our mission is to make data loss and data tampering a thing of the past.

Using a blockchain to back up your data makes a redundant copy for each device on your network. That means that to take down your data, an attacker or any unauthorized user would have to delete every single copy of your database from every device on the network. Furthermore, because of the nature of the blockchain, any changes made are immediately visible, allowing Jericho to function as a built in version control for your database.
